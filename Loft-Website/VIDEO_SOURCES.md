## üìã What to Look For

### ‚úÖ Ideal Video Characteristics:
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080) or higher
- **Orientation:** Landscape/horizontal (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Duration:** 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **Style:** Slow motion, minimal movement, cinematic
- **Colors:** Natural greens, earthy tones, or muted colors
- **File Size:** Try to keep under 5-10MB for web optimization

### ‚ùå Avoid:
- Videos with fast, jarring movements
- Heavy text or logos in the video
- Portrait/vertical videos
- Videos with people's faces prominently featured
- Very bright or high-contrast videos that clash with text

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Download Multiple Options:** Get 2-3 videos so you can test which looks best
2. **Test File Sizes:** Smaller files load faster but may lose quality
3. **Consider Mood:** Match the video's mood to your brand (premium, calm, professional)
4. **Color Match:** Look for videos with greens similar to your brand color (#1B4D3E)
5. **Movement Level:** Subtle movements work best with text overlays
